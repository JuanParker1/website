"Hoot, hoot" - she added, and now I was running forward with all the pep, squirting urine at every step. And finally we jumped out into a moonlit clearing, and she made a 180, perched upon a tree leering. I was lucky, and my camp was near, legend has it that she's still out there filling the woods with fear. So if if you're ever out there, and hear a hoot, better grab onto your butt, and scoot.
