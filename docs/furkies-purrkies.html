Furkies Purrkies

Anthology of Inspirational Rhyme

Just another dang old Audio Book about wisdom and growing up. It is not recommended that you read it, or listen to it. But you are challenged to make your own, one that is much better, and much wiser.

Each day, I receive hundreds of emails asking what a Furkey is. Furkey is defined as a fluffy thought on the subject of a cute creature, often preceding the petting of one. For completion sake, a Purrkey in context of Animal Medicine has to deal with explosive animal flatulence, that requently results in rousing the animal to a higher state of consiousness. In the context that is used here, however; it means the percolation of wise thoughts on a myriad of subjects.

#174: Web Pages For Ages

I've been making web pages for ages, learning slowly in many stages. I begun in 1999, and I am still learning new things about design. All the programming languages changed many times, but knowing the whole history is very useful at times. I've learned a lot from the command line, to art, colors, design, and how to reach the finish line. I am surprised how long it all took, but with all I know I am sure I could write a whole book. I still have to learn a bunch of stuff, but it is not going to be very tough. I mostly have to get all the colors, and glows to dance and blend, I kind of left all the fun stuff for the very end.
